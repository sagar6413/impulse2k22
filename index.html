Dance Battle


Rules

  • This is an inter-collegiate event, accepting all type of performances (Solo, Duo & Group).
  • Reporting time to the event and dance sequence of the competition must be strictly followed by the participants. The participants should arrive atleast an hour before their performance.
  • Event duration:( respective time)
  • The time limit of the performance will be 4+2 minutes from empty stage to empty stage.
  • Stage Specifications : stage area 20ft x 22ft (approx).
  • All costumes and accessories have to be arranged by the participating team. Naked flames and live animals are not allowed.
  • The participating teams are requested to ensure that their presentation is in keeping with dignity of the fest.
  • The teams should carry an official letter from their respective institutes signed by the competent authority, mentioning the name of participants.
  • Participants should get their songs/music on a pen-drive. It is advised that they have a backup in CD. Team !MPULSE will not be responsible for this.
  • Marks shall be deducted for exceeding the time limit (20% deducted for exceeding 6 minutes and team might be disqualified for exceeding 8 minutes based on judge’s discretion).
  • Any props or other materials that have to be used will have to be managed by the participating team itself. The organizers will not provide any properties or materials.
  • In case of malfunctioning of any equipment provided, the team will be allowed to repeat their performance from the beginning or from the point of disruption that the team wishes, with the consent of the judge.
  • If the participating team does not show up at their allocated time then the team will be disqualified. In case of unavoidable circumstances please contact the organizers. The participation fee will not be refunded under any circumstances.
  • Decisions made by the organizers and judges will be binding and final.
